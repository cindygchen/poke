DESPERATION

Oh wait...we mean motivation. Our team was connected by our love for San Diego and food. We all enjoy using Yelp to search for food but it lacks our need to search for things to do. We decided to create a web application that would deliver what we believe to be the ultimate solution.


HOW CAN WE HELP?

Helps visualize activities in San Diego, whether you're just looking to grab a quick bite, or plan a full itinerary with some friends


WHO IS THIS MADE FOR?

Anybody and everybody!
Well...those interested in San Diegan activities of course.

OUR DESIGN PROCESS

  • BRAINSTORMIN TIME

    We began to brainstorm project ideas. What was our motivation? What kind of APIs could we use?

  • BATTLE OF THE APIS

    We narrowed down to two ideas and decided that we would choose based on who could make the API for each project to work first. That was the begining of a karaoke webpage.

  • PEN AND PAPER
  • LET THE STRUGGLES BEGIN

    Once we dug deeper into the Spotify API, we realized that the object that it returned did not contain the full music video but rather a 30 second preview.

  • Back to square one.
  • NEW BEGININGS

    We decided to table our karaoke idea and come up with a new idea.

  • WHEELS ARE CHURNING

    APIs are finally working in our favor. Now we've also began to work on the front end.

  • AND FINALLY

    We present to you GREGIS.
